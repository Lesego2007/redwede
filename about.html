About YouthConnect SA

Founded in 2019 in Johannesburg, YouthConnect SA empowers youth aged 15-30 to bridge the gap between education and employment through career counseling, skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship support.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every young person has the skills and opportunities to thrive.
